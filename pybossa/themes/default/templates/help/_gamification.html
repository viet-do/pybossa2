Gamification

Term Simplification

You can help us simplify terms and sentences. You will look at complex terms or sentences and try to put them in plain language. Working on these tasks can give you badges and XP to rank up.

Ranks and XP

Rank shows how well a user completes tasks. Every time you complete a task, you gain Experience Points (XP). When you reach a higher number of points, you can earn a new rank. You will earn cool badges to show on your profile. The higher your rank, the more XP you need to gain a new rank.

Badges

Badges are special achievement stickers that you can earn. You can earn badges for doing tasks, checking web resources, or just by reading this page! Once you complete a special feat, you will earn a colorful sticker. It will show on your profile for everyone to see.

Leaderboard

The Leaderboard shows all users who help in the Easytext.org app. You can check ranks and badge counts. You can see how many terms/sentences a person has simplified.
